Engineering Assumptions & Defaults
  • 2D Projection: The 3D mechanics described in the dossier are projected onto a 2D plane (Z-axis = 0) for visualization purposes.
  • Scale: 1 pixel â‰ˆ 100 meters. Visual inputs are scaled for usability (ships are drawn larger than physics size).

  • Formula Implementation: Formulas are implemented exactly as defined in Section 2 of the dossier, including clamping and exponential factors.
